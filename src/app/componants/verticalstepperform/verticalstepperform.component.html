Showcase Your Profile…impress the potential clients

  • Curate your Profile:
    • Profile Picture: Your name, and include a high resolution professional looking photo of your face. This will help to ensure potential clients’ trust that you’re a real person, in addition to making your profile appear more professional
    • Experiences: Include your areas of expertise, experiences, or anything else you think is important - keep it relevant!
    • Languages: what languages you speak and your level of fluency; this is incredibly important if your gig includes skills like writing or translating or work with multi-national teams
    • Location: Being flexible on the location of your Gig will be an advantage on opportunity of a match
  • A brief and catchy motto or tagline of yourself could attract the client to want to read/know more about you.
  • Develop and establish a credible portfolio of your past work and professional experiences, projects you have completed successfully
  • Any referrals or testimonials from past clients will increase your opportunities

Education Details

  • List all your relevant educational details, both the formal qualifications as well as any certificate courses you took, to increase your competency in your field of expertise. More than 80% of experts and professionals on GigXGlobal have some form of advanced degrees or professional certification.
  • List your educational and professional history
  • Strengthen your competencies and capabilities by including other activities both in the professional and social spaces; set yourself apart from other talents applying for the job

Work Experience

  • Don’t be shy to list your relevant work experiences to strengthen your profile
  • Show that you are the heavyweight talent that the client should engage
  • Include specific project examples that may be relevant to the client’s needs as well as any that will show that you are exceptional for the position
  • Impress the clients with your portfolios of work - make yourself stand out from the crowd

Job Preference

  • Skills: Highlight your key skills and competencies. Include any skills applicable to the gigs you intend to apply for/interested in. It should include abilities you’ve gained through past jobs, hobbies, or other life experiences
  • Add approximately 10 skills, be specific about the skill-sets you have. Choose wisely what skills you wish to showcase.This will also help GigXGlobal’s back end technology to read and identify your true areas of expertise. The ‘narrower’ the skills tagging, the better the % of job match accuracy
  • Include general skills (eg: Business Strategy, Project Management, Marketing, etc) and specific skills (Eg: Market Research with focus on Market sizing OR Investment Strategy with a focus on Investment Analysis)
