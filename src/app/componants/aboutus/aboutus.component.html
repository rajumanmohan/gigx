Gigxglobal.com is a fractional work platform that specialises in enabling the gig economy. The GigxGlobal Story: Is there a platform for agile businesses and professional talent for the gig economy?

It all started with our own need to connect to fractional work and offering our expertise to businesses who have fractional work for highly skilled talent to offer but can’t find them. Our journey lead us into many conversations with business owners, CEOs, HR Professionals, professionals groups and associations, individual experts globally. There was a common theme, the needs were absolutely real, a talent platform for experts on demand is sorely missing AND much needed!

Why do we feel strongly about this? Validation of the gaps we were addressing from the market and of course our own gut feel. Our research and analysis of the trends out there, got us thinking even deeper into the solution we would build.

We saw companies today still struggling with talent challenges at multiple levels, thus incurring significant costs and risks. With the new economy being here, and the available technologies and tools today, work is being redefined significantly. It is now possible to work from anywhere in the world, nurture our own passions and work is no longer a physical place. It can happen from anywhere, any space and anytime. The platform would be the enabler. It would be the way professional will work. It is Work of the Future!

In 2018 co-founders Shareen Dato’ Abdul Ghani and Zuraidah Mian, pioneers and the original creators GigxGlobal.com got together to design the solution. We had a mission and a desire to change the landscape of how profession also do their work. We saw the trends, we understood the needs, we knew the solution would transform, not just disrupt, and drive this exciting new ways of Work of the Future.

Subsequently, we turned the team into a powerhouse of experts when Dato’ Munirah Looi, Kartina Abdul Latif and Yugandhar Raghu, joined us as the founding members.

Dato’ Munirah Looi is the founder and CEO of Brandt International, a leading business and customer experience solutions provider that provides consulting, customer service, sales, and digital workforce solutions. Kartina is a business consultant who has spent 22 years consulting to various businesses, governments and not-for-profits around the ASEAN region on the execution of their strategic transformation initiatives, corporate strategies, human capital transformation and large-scale complex programme management.

With their combined wealth of expertise and market savvy business acumen and connectivity, the founding members will bring their business expertise and value proposition directly to clients in reimagining new ways of work using digital business platforms.
