About Gigx

GigX is a work marketplace platform that specializes in connecting and facilitating between talent demand and supply with the aim to manage talents in the age of uncertainty by providing strategic workforce solutions through the XFactors for companies to find the right talent and for talents to find the right gig opportunities

Our Objectives

  • Connect and facilitate talent demand and supply on Future of Work especially for the gig economy and contingent workforce in one marketplace.
  • Match between gig/project to a competent contingent worker

Our Features

Profiling of talent, firms and gigs.

Performance rating mechanism for both talent and firms.

Accreditation as safety nets for gig talent e.g. retirement savings and other benefits.

Exciting events on current and upcoming trends, networking with experienced community
